Our Famous Roasts:

Black Oak.

Black Oak Roast is sourced from the high-altitude Black Oak forests in the Pacific Northwest, known for their rich biodiversity. Handpicked and sun-dried, these beans offer a bold, complex flavor with notes of dark chocolate and toasted nuts, and a deep, smoky aroma.

Prairie Gold.

Discover a medium roast coffee that embodies the essence of the outdoors. Grown under vast prairie skies, this blend offers a rich flavor with notes of toasted nuts and hints of wildflowers. Perfect for those who seek a taste of nature in every cup.

Sunkissed Blend.

Introducing Tropical Sunkiss, a light roast coffee that brings the taste of the tropics to your cup. Grown under the warm, sun-drenched skies, this blend offers a smooth and bright flavor with notes of tropical fruits and a hint of sunshine in every sip. Perfect for those who crave a taste of paradise in their morning brew.

Direct Trade. Ethically Sourced. Sustainable Practices.

At our company, ethical sourcing is at the heart of everything we do. We are committed to ensuring that the farmers who grow our coffee beans are treated fairly and are properly compensated for their hard work.

Through direct trade relationships, we work closely with farmers to ensure that they receive a fair price for their beans, allowing them to invest in their farms and communities.

We prioritize sustainable farming practices to protect the environment and ensure the long-term viability of coffee farming.

With every cup of coffee, you can feel good knowing that you are supporting ethical and sustainable practices that benefit both farmers and the planet.
